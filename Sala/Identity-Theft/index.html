IDENTITY THEFT PROTECTION

Identity theft has been one of the top consumer complaints filed with the FTC for more than 15 years.*

ID Theft Defense℠, offered by Prepaid Legal Services, Inc., understands all the potential threats, how to protect against them, and how to restore any damage done.

  • • Providing counseling and emergency assistance, if needed
  • • Restoring your identity should a breach occur
